# Local Currency & Pricing Guide (Post-Morocco)

This guide provides practical pricing examples to help gauge cash needs in Mauritania, Senegal, and The Gambia. Prices are approximate estimates based on recent traveler reports (2025/2026 context).

## Morocco (Dirham - MAD)
*   **Exchange Rate**: ~10-11 MAD = €1.
*   **Pricing Scale**:
    *   **Coffee**: 15-20 MAD (€1.50 - €2.00)
    *   **Simple Meal (Tagine)**: 40-70 MAD (€4.00 - €7.00)
    *   **Restaurant Meal**: 100-200 MAD (€10 - €20)
    *   **Budget Hotel**: 200-400 MAD (€20 - €40)
    *   **Fuel (Diesel)**: ~12-14 MAD/L (€1.20/L)
*   **Credit Cards**: **Widely Accepted** in cities, hotels, and major gas stations (Afriquia, Shell). Cash needed for souks and rural areas.

## Mauritania (Ouguiya - MRU)
*   **Exchange Rate**: ~50 MRU = £1 / €1.20 (Highly variable black market vs official)
*   **Pricing Scale**:
    *   **Coffee/Tea**: 30-50 MRU (€0.70 - €1.20)
    *   **Simple Meal (Street/Local)**: 150-250 MRU (€3.50 - €6.00)
    *   **Restaurant Meal**: 400-800 MRU (€9.00 - €18.00)
    *   **Budget Hotel**: 1,500-2,500 MRU (€35 - €60)
    *   **Fuel (Diesel)**: ~38-40 MRU/L (€0.90/L) - *Expensive & poor quality*
*   **Credit Cards**: **Cities Only** (Nouakchott/Nouadhibou - Rare elsewhere. Cash is King).

## Senegal (West African CFA Franc - XOF)
*   **Exchange Rate**: Fixed to Euro (1 € = 655.957 XOF).
*   **Pricing Scale**:
    *   **Coffee**: 1,000 XOF (€1.50)
    *   **Simple Meal (Street)**: 1,500-3,000 XOF (€2.30 - €4.50)
    *   **Restaurant Meal**: 6,000-12,000 XOF (€9 - €18)
    *   **Budget Hotel**: 20,000-35,000 XOF (€30 - €53)
    *   **Beer (Gazelle)**: 1,000-2,000 XOF (€1.50 - €3.00)
*   **Credit Cards**: **Cities** (Dakar/Saint-Louis/Saly. Cash required for fuel/tolls/rural areas).

## The Gambia (Dalasi - GMD)
*   **Exchange Rate**: ~80-85 GMD = £1 (Volatile).
*   **Pricing Scale**:
    *   **Coffee**: 100-200 GMD (€1.50 - €3.00)
    *   **Simple Meal**: 150-300 GMD (€2.20 - €4.50)
    *   **Restaurant Meal**: 600-1,200 GMD (€9 - €18)
    *   **Budget Hotel**: 1,500-3,000 GMD (€22 - €45)
    *   **Beer (Julbrew)**: 100-150 GMD (€1.50 - €2.20)
*   **Credit Cards**: **Cities** (Banjul/Senegambia Strip - Hotels/Upscale Restaurants only. Cash economy otherwise).
