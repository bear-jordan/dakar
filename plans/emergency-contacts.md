# Emergency Contacts Research Plan

**Objective**: Add a concise "Emergency & Medical" section to each country guide (`docs/<country>.md`) immediately following the preamble.

**Scope**:
*   **National Emergency Numbers**: Police, Ambulance, Fire.
*   **Key Regional Hospitals**: 1-2 reputable hospitals (private/Western-standard preferred) along the route.
*   **Embassy Contacts**: US/UK Embassy phone numbers in the capital.

## Phase 1: Data Gathering
**Source**: Web Search (US State Dept, UK FCDO, IAMAT).

**Target Data for Each Country**:
1.  **Numbers**: Police, Ambulance.
2.  **Hospital**: Name + City of best trauma/emergency care near our route (e.g., Clinique Internationale in Marrakech/Nouakchott).
3.  **Embassy**: Emergency line.

## Phase 2: Integration
**Format**:
```markdown
## Appendix: Emergency & Medical
*   **Police**: [Number] | **Ambulance**: [Number]
*   **Key Hospital**: [Name, City] (Best for trauma/serious issues).
*   **Embassy**: [Country] Embassy [City]: [Phone Number]
```
