# Europe Transit (France & Spain) (~Jan 16 -- Jan 19 2026)
*   **Start Date**: ~Jan 16, 2026 (Based on 21-day trip duration ending Feb 6)
*   **End Date**: ~Jan 19, 2026
*   **Duration**: ~3-4 Days
*   **Visa**: Schengen (Visa-free <90 days for US/UK)

## 1. France
*   **Driving Requirements**:
    *   **Kit**: Warning triangle, reflective jackets (accessible from *inside* car), breathalyzer (technically required but fines suspended), headlight converters, UK sticker.
    *   **Speed**: Radar traps common.
    *   **Crit-Air**: Emissions sticker required for entering certain cities (Paris, Rouen, etc.). Avoid cities or get sticker online in advance.
    *   **Tolls**: Expensive. Credit cards accepted.
*   **Fuel**: Expensive. Fill up before highway if possible.

## 2. Spain
*   **Driving Requirements**:
    *   **Kit**: **TWO** warning triangles, reflective jackets (in car), spare bulb kit, UK sticker.
    *   **New 2026**: V16 Flashing Warning Beacon (magnetic orange light) replaces triangles eventually.
    *   **Speed**: Radar traps. Spot fines for foreigners (cash/card).
*   **Route to Tarifa**:
    *   Long drive (700+ miles from border).
    *   **Tolls**: Some "Peage" roads towards south.

## 3. Gibraltar (Optional Stop)
*   **Status**: British Overseas Territory.
*   **Border**: Hard border. Exits Schengen zone. Queues can be long.
*   **Fuel**: Tax-free (cheaper than Spain).
*   **Logistics**: Park outside and walk in to avoid queue, unless fueling.
