# Europe Transit (UK to Spain) (~Jan 15 -- Jan 21 2026)
*   **Start Date**: Jan 15, 2026 (Portsmouth)
*   **End Date**: Jan 21, 2026 (Ferry to Morocco)
*   **Duration**: ~6 Days (2 Days Ferry + 3 Days Spain)
*   **Distance**: ~1,000 km (Santander to Cadiz)
*   **Drive Load**: ~5-6 Hours/Day (Relaxed)
*   **Visa**: Schengen (Visa-free <90 days for US/UK)
*   **Weather**: **Cold**. Santander (10°C). Central Spain (Madrid) can be **FREEZING**. South (16°C).

## 1. The Route: Brittany Ferries
*   **Departure**: **Jan 15 @ 23:30 GMT** (Portsmouth Guildhall).
*   **Arrival**: **Jan 17 @ 08:00 GMT** (Santander).
*   **Vessel**: Brittany Ferries (likely *Santoña* or *Galicia*).
*   **Logistics**:
    *   Relax, sleep, and avoid the toll/fuel costs of France.
    *   Bring sea-sickness tablets (Bay of Biscay can be rough in winter).

## 2. Spain Drive (Jan 17 - Jan 19)
*   **Route**: Santander -> Burgos -> Seville -> Cadiz.
*   **Distance**: ~1,000 km.
*   **Conditions**: Excellent highways ("Autovia"). Mostly toll-free.
*   **Hazards**:
    *   **Winter**: Risk of snow in the north (Cantabrian Mountains) and near Madrid.
    *   **V16 Beacon**: New 2026 requirement (Orange flashing magnetic light) instead of triangles.
*   **Stopover**:
    *   You have Jan 17 & 18 to cover this distance.
    *   *Suggestion*: Stop in **Salamanca** or **Seville** for tapas.

## 3. The Rendezvous: Cadiz/Tarifa
*   **Meeting Point**: **Mesón de Sancho** (Ctra. N.340 - Km 94, 11380, Cádiz).
*   **Check-in**: **Jan 19** (15:00 CET).
*   **Stay**: Jan 19 - Jan 21 (2 Nights).
*   **Activities**:
    *   Pre-Sahara briefings.
    *   Car prep/sticker application.
    *   "Shit Shirts Night" (Traditional rally event).
*   **Departure**: **Jan 21 @ 09:00 CET** (Tangier Express from Algeciras).

## Fun Things to Do (Spain)
*   **Seville Cathedral** [Cultural]
    *   *Description*: Largest Gothic cathedral in the world. Christopher Columbus's tomb.
    *   *Logistics*: On the route south from Santander.

*   **Roman Bridge of Salamanca** [History]
    *   *Description*: Stunning ancient bridge in a vibrant university city.
    *   *Logistics*: Great halfway stop.
