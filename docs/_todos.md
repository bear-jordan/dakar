# Trip Action Items

## Immediate Priority
- [x] **Passport Check**: Ensure passport is valid for at least 6 months beyond the planned return date.
- [x] **Vaccinations**: Get Yellow Fever vaccination and ensure you have the **Yellow Card** certificate (Required for Mauritania/Senegal).
- [x] **Mauritania**: Apply for E-Visa online (do not wait for the border).
- [ ] **Gibraltar/UK**: Apply for UK ETA (Electronic Travel Authorisation) if traveling after Jan 8, 2025.
- [x] **Admin Database**: Upload V5C (Red/Inside pages), Passport Bio, and E-Visa to "The Buck" database.

## Documentation to Print
- [ ] **Fiches**: Print ~50-100 copies of the "Fiche" (personal/car info sheet). *Tip: Print 4 per page*.
- [ ] **Passport**: Colour copies of main page.
- [ ] **Car Documents**: Colour copies of V5/Registration.
- [ ] **Driving Licence**: Colour copies.
- [ ] **Insurance**: Physical copies of green cards/insurance docs.

## Vehicle Compliance (Mandatory Law)
- [ ] **France**: Purchase **Crit-Air Sticker** online (allow shipping time).
- [ ] **Spain**: Purchase **V16 Flashing Beacon** (new 2026 requirement).
- [ ] **Europe**: Buy **2x Warning Triangles** and **High-Vis Jackets** (one per seat, keep in cabin).
- [ ] **Europe**: Apply **UK Sticker** to rear of car (if UK reg).
- [ ] **Europe**: Fit **Headlamp Converters** (Beam deflectors).
- [ ] **Africa**: Mount **Fire Extinguisher** in cabin (accessible).
- [ ] **Africa**: Purchase **Tow Rope** (Mandatory for towing rules).

## Vehicle & Logistics
- [x] **Senegal**: Secure "Senegal 1 Car Pass" (contact organizers/Dave).
- [ ] **Cash**: Withdraw/Exchange Euros (small denominations) for border fees (Mauritania/Senegal/Gambia).

- [ ] **Sand Ladders**: Buy **RIGID** ladders (MaxTrax style). *Do not buy carpet*.
